The work

An editorial, scoped to the place.

We arrive after the work of looking is already done. Linger is a planned narrative, built around what the property is most itself in. Sometimes that is its architecture and its light. Sometimes a ritual. Sometimes the figures who move through it.

Each Linger is shaped to its property. The form follows the place.

Scope

Defined per project.

Linger is scoped in conversation. Duration, casting, styling, and final delivery are shaped to the editorial, not the other way around.
