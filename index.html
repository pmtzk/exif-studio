Before you reach out

Common questions.

Is this just a photography service?

No. Photography is one possible output. The work begins with a commercial read of the property and only then decides what needs to change.

Do we have to commit to the full project?

No. The Audit stands on its own. If you continue into a Visual Reset, the Audit fee is credited toward the project.

How much time does it require from our team?

The Audit uses public channels and requires no team time. The Reset needs access for the shoot and one brief alignment conversation.

What does it cost?

Pricing is set per property based on size and channel count. You receive a specific number in the first reply, before any commitment.
