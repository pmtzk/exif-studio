What EXIF reviews

The Audit reads the property as a connected system.

Activate each layer to see how one decision affects the next. Not every Audit includes every channel.

The property itself

Its concept, setting, physical character and intended position.

The current presentation

Website, booking pages, OTA listings, social channels and selected brand materials.

The existing image library

What works, repeats, is missing or no longer reflects the place.

The competitive context

How comparable properties present their difference.

The next investment

Photography, redesign, repositioning, launch or broader change.

The Audit reads them together.
